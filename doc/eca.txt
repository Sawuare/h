NAME
  eca.py - paint elementary cellular automata on the framebuffer

SYNOPSIS
  eca.py

DESCRIPTION
  eca.py requires permission to read and write the Linux framebuffer device /dev/fb0,
  and that might require being the superuser or being in the video group.

  eca.py must be executed in the Linux console (i.e. TERM=linux).
