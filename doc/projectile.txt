NAME
  projectile.py - analyze dragless projectile motion on Earth

SYNOPSIS
  projectile.py

DESCRIPTION
  The program prompts for the following launch details of a projectile.

    1. Angle in degrees.
    2. Speed in meters per second.

  Then it prints the following flight details of the projectile, assuming no drag and
  a standard acceleration due to gravity of 9.80665 meters per second per second.

    1. Peak in meters.
    2. Range in meters.
    3. Duration in seconds.
