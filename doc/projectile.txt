NAME
  projectile - analyze dragless projectile motion on Earth

SYNOPSIS
  projectile

DESCRIPTION
  projectile prompts for these launch details of a projectile:

    1. Angle in degrees
    2. Speed in meters per second

  Then it prints these flight details of the projectile, assuming no drag and a
  standard acceleration due to gravity of 9.80665 meters per second per second:

    1. Peak in meters
    2. Range in meters
    3. Duration in seconds
