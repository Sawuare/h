NAME
  qda - a quantitative data analyzer

SYNOPSIS
  qda

DESCRIPTION
  qda reads a line of space-separated numbers then prints the following statistics
  with up to 10 significant digits:

    Count
    Total
    Minimum
    Maximum
    Range
    Mid-Range
    First Quartile
    Second Quartile
    Third Quartile
    Interquartile Range
    Mid-Hinge
    Arithmetic Mean
    Geometric Mean
    Standard Deviation
    Skewness
    Kurtosis
