NAME
  qda - a quantitative data analyzer

SYNOPSIS
  qda

DESCRIPTION
  qda reads space-separated numbers then prints the following statistics with up to
  10 significant digits:

    Count
    Sum
    Minimum
    Maximum
    Range
    Mid-Range
    First Quartile
    Second Quartile
    Third Quartile
    Interquartile Range
    Mid-Hinge
    Arithmetic Mean
    Geometric Mean
    Standard Deviation
    Skewness
    Kurtosis
